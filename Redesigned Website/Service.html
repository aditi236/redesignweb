Painting Services

Our Services.

Over the years, we have kept pace with the latest technological developments and adapted our painting services to best meet the needs of our clients.

This allows us to comfortably move from painting industrial plants and machines to boardrooms; from restoring historical landmarks to applying the newest specialty coatings; and from traditional surface preparation to lead paint removal.

Our Painting Services include -

  • Surface Preparation.
  • Restoration Services.
  • Lead Paint Abatement.
  • Aerial work.
  • Protective Painting.
  • Corrosion Services and many other services.

Specialties.

Our painting specialties cover a broad range of industry sectors, by customer demand; we have a specialized industrial division, which service all types of Industrial structures, buildings, factories, and manufacturing plants.

Structures.

In architecture, construction, engineering and real estate development the word building may refer to one of the following: Any man-made structure used or intended for supporting or sheltering any use or continuous occupancy or an act of construction.

Buildings and other structures that are not intended for continuous human occupancy are nonbuilding structures or simply structures.

Contractors.

General painting contractors are considered to be "generalists" and speciality painting contractors are "specialists." If you need a specific type of building or structure painted, naturally, you'll go with a specialist.
